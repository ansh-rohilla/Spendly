Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Spendly collects, uses, and protects information when you use our personal expense tracking application. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

To provide the service, Spendly collects only the information needed to power its features. This includes:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored in hashed form) when you create an account.
  • Expense data — the transactions, categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions you choose to log in the app.
  • Usage data — basic technical information such as your browser type and device type, used to keep the service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Store, retrieve, and display the expense records you create.
  • Generate the summaries, totals, and category breakdowns you request.
  • Diagnose issues and prevent abuse of the service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected with reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. Expense data is associated with your account and is only accessible to you while you are signed in.

You may view, update, or delete your stored data at any time from your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, your expense records are permanently removed from our active systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small set of trusted third party providers to operate. These may include a hosting provider for our servers, an email service for account notifications, and an analytics provider for aggregate usage statistics. We share only the minimum information required for these providers to perform their function, and we select providers that meet industry-standard security practices.

These third parties are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than providing services to Spendly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to correct inaccurate information, and to request deletion of your account and associated records. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the support options provided in the app.
